## Industrial Inspection Case Study
The Zhang-Suen skeletonization technique can be applied in industrial inspection to extract the skeletal structure of objects from images. In this case study, we used the technique to inspect the quality of metal parts. We captured images of the parts using a high-resolution camera and applied the Zhang-Suen skeletonization algorithm to extract the skeletal structure of the parts. The resulting skeletons were then compared to a reference skeleton to detect any defects or irregularities. The algorithm was able to accurately detect defects such as cracks and scratches, and the inspection process was significantly faster and more accurate than manual inspection.
